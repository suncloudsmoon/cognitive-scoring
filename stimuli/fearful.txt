She heard the floorboards creak in the hallway outside her bedroom door. The house was supposed to be empty. She held her breath and watched the doorknob slowly begin to turn in the darkness.

The hikers realized they were lost when the trail disappeared into thick fog. Their phones had no signal, and the temperature was dropping rapidly. Strange sounds echoed through the trees around them.

The warning sirens blared across the city as the sky turned an unnatural shade of green. People ran through the streets, looking for shelter. The ground began to shake, and a deep rumbling sound grew louder every second.

He was swimming alone in the ocean when he felt something large brush against his leg underwater. He looked down but could see nothing in the murky depths below. Then he felt it again, circling closer.

The elevator stopped suddenly between floors, and the lights went out. She pressed every button, but nothing happened. Then she heard breathing in the darkness, and realized she was not alone in the elevator.
